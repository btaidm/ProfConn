Access your Home Drive from OS X!

A PDF copy of the instructions can be downloaded here for your convenience.

Step 1 - Setting Up Software

  1. Open either Safari or Firefox
    1. Make sure that you have the latest version of Java installed. Most computers have Java and regularly update it, but if yours does not it will cause problems.
    2. This process doesn’t work through Google Chrome and other browsers.
  2. Go to vpn.mtu.edu
  3. Enter your Michigan Tech ISO Username and Password
  4. Under "Network Access" , click "Get an MTU address"
  5. A Window will appear that will say: "Installing Network Access for the first time"
  6. Select installation method: download and run. Save the file.
  7. Go to "Downloads folder" , and click the file "SSLVpn.pkg.tgz" .
  8. It will extract a file called “mac_sslvpn.pkg” . Click that file and run it.
  9. Installer will open. Install the package.
    1. It may ask you "Install for which drive?" Use the default drive or whatever drive you use for installing applications.
    2. It probably will ask for an Admin Password. Type the password.

  10. Quit your webrowser completely and restart it.
    1. “X-ing” out the browser windows will not quit the program, you need to press Command(⌘)-Q or go to Safari → Quit Safari on the toolbar at the top of your screen.
    2. Bookmarking this site and vpn.mtu.edu can be helpful for this step. You could also copy the URL for this page and paste it into the address bar of your browser when you restart it.

Step 2 - Connecting to Michigan Tech

  1. Go to vpn.mtu.edu and log back in
  2. Enter your Michigan Tech ISO username and password
  3. Click "Get an MTU address"
    1. This time the windows will connect to Michigan Tech VPN instead of asking you to install a program
  4. Leave that window open
    1. If you close it, you will lose your connect to the Michigan Tech Network.
    2. Minimizing the window will not end the connection

Step 3 - Connecting to the Home Drive

  1. Goto "Finder"
  2. On the menu bar at the very top of your screen, click "Go", then "Connect to Server" from the menu
    1. This can be done by pressing ⌘-K
  3. Copy and Paste the following into the "Server Address" slot:

    smb://multidrive.iso.mtu.edu

  4. In the next window that pops up, make sure "Register User" is selected. Enter "MTU\" in the "Name" slot, followed by your Michigan Tech Username (no space between them). Then enter your Michigan Tech Password in the password slot.
    1. For example, if your username is "username", you would type in “MTU\username”
    2. Make sure that you use the correct slash mark; it’s “\” , and not “/” .
  5. Press "Connect"
    1. It is suggested that you check the "Remember Password" box to speed up this process in the future.
  6. It will ask you what volume you to mount, "macmulti" and "multidrive." Select "macmulti"
  7. You will see a window with an icon named "multidrivemounter-1.0.dmg". Click it.
  8. When a window opens, drag the Multidrive Mounter icon to your Desktop or folder of your choice to copy it.
    1. There is also a Readme file. Looking at it is optional

  9. Click the Multidrive mounter icon.
  10. It will ask for your Michigan Tech Username, enter it
    1. The "MTU\" is not needed
      2. here
  11. Whena window pops up with server choices, select mtucifs_home, and press OK
  12. You are now connected to your home drive. Use Finder to navigate to your drive

Connecting to the Home Drive afterwords

After you log out or restart your computer, you will be disconnected form the homedrive. These steps tell you how to re-connect without going through a lengthy installaion process and restarting yur browser
  1. Connect to the Michigan Tech Network following the steps from step 2.
  2. Use the Multidrive Mounter to connect to mtucifs_home. This time it will ask you for your password. You can select for your computer to remember your password.
  3. You are now connected to your home drive. Use Finder to navigate to your drive
